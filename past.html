Past Projects

Flood Relief

During the 2020 rainy season, Central Vietnam suffered immensely with flooding and storms. Villagers faced a prolonged loss of electricity, running water and access to food. Da Nang Go saw there was an imminent need for a fundraiser and action in order to support the people in these villages. Click here to read more about this project!

Christmas Shoebox Donations

At the end of 2020, Da Nang Go collected Christmas Shoebox Donations and gave them to children who don't normally receive any presents. Click here to read more about this project and how you can get involved next Christmas!
