Volunteering Projects

What we do

Da Nang Go has created multiple projects to help those in need in Central Vietnam.

We aim to provide relief to those in need by reasons of youth, age, ill-health, disability, financial hardship or other disadvantages

Our Projects

VAVA

​The Vietnam Association for the Victims of Agent Orange

VAVA Da Nang (DAVA) has two day-care centres for children who have been affected by Agent Orange. Many of the children here suffer from mental and physical disabilities which prevent them from attending school. Da Nang Go Volunteers provide fun and productive workshops on a regular basis. Click here to read more about our volunteer program at DAVA and how to sign up!

Hannah's Soup Kitchen

If you frequent Da Nang's An Thuong area, you may know Hannah as the owner of Hannah's Pub. As well as serving up hearty meals for her customers, Hannah runs a Soup Kitchen for those in need. On Mondays and Tuesdays Da Nang Go volunteers help Hannah to prepare up to 100 free meals for disadvantaged local residents and the children of the Cancer Ward at 600 Beds Hospital, Da Nang. Click here to find out more information and how to volunteer with Hannah!

Street Children Program of Da Nang

Da Nang has four orphanages located near the heart of the expat community. Once a week, Da Nang Go sends volunteers to entertain the children through English classes, activities or garden projects. Click here to find out more information and how to volunteer with the orphanages!

Passerelles Numériques Vietnam

Passerelles Numériques Vietnam focuses on providing educational and professional training in the digital sector to young underprivileged people. Da Nang Go supports their English Club by providing English speaking volunteers to assist and mentor students in their weekly English Club. Click here to find out more information and how to volunteer!
